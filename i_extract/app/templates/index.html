Local LLM-Powered Data Extraction: From Documents to Structured Data
Upload a PDF or JPEG and extract structured key-value pairs—names, dates, amounts, IDs—using a local LLM. The split-view interface lets you review and edit extracted fields, click a row to locate the source text, and export results as CSV. All processing stays on your infrastructure.

How to Use This Demo
  1. Upload a document — Select a PDF or JPEG file and click Extract Data.
  2. Review the results — Extracted key-value pairs appear in the right pane. Labels and values are editable inline.
  3. Locate source text — Click any row to highlight the matching value in the document pane on the left (PDF only; not available for JPEG images).
  4. Export or refine — Add, edit, or delete fields as needed, then click Download CSV.
Content you upload may be used for model training and fine-tuning.
